When writing brain entries, keep them terse and as few words as possible to get the point across.
- text: a single-line OPEN question, starting with what / how / why / which. Never a yes/no question (does / is / are / should / can): those presume the answer and cannot be split.
- answer: a concise and clear explanation of the findings. Directly reference and quote citations.
Don't narrate the write in your reply.
