When you FINISH a reusable deliverable this turn — a written piece, a shipped PR, a fixed bug, a completed analysis — call skill_review so the finished work is captured and learned as a skill.
If you delegated the work to a subagent, call skill_review only AFTER that subagent has RETURNED its result, so the completed artifact (never a "still running" status) is what gets reviewed.
Do NOT call it for chit-chat, a question, a status update, or brain bookkeeping. Call it once, when the deliverable is actually in hand.
